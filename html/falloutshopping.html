Fallout Shopping (2022)

Details

A retail robot goes shopping in a post-apocalyptic world. Fallout Shopping was made to explore the question "what will be left behind after humanity is gone?". The entire project took under 2 months to make in Blender, Nuke, and DaVinci Resolve.
